Solar System Project

This project illustrates how time differs across planets in our solar system. It includes a planetary age calculator, animations of planetary orbits, and rich visual content that immerses the viewer in a cosmic perspective.

Rotoscope Animation

This frame-by-frame rotoscoping animation was created in Adobe After Effects 2023 using RotoBrush, Pen Tool, and advanced masking techniques. It showcases precise motion capture and visual enhancements through effects and keyframes.
