About the Project

This rotoscope animation project involves frame-by-frame editing to isolate and stylize motion. It was done entirely using Adobe After Effects 2023 tools, combining manual brush work, keyframes, effects, and layering techniques.

Tools Used

RotoBrush Tool

The Roto Brush tool allows you to separate a subject from its background by painting strokes over the desired object. It enables a semi-automated way to create complex cutouts frame by frame.

Pen Tool

The Pen Tool offers full control for creating precise masks, paths, and custom shapes. It was used to refine mask edges and draw graphical elements.

Effects & Presets Panel

This panel provided quick access to hundreds of effects like Fill, Glow, and Tint. Applying presets allowed for fast experimentation and improved the animation's appeal.

Transform Properties + Stopwatch

Used for keyframing properties like Position, Scale, and Opacity to animate movements like zooming and fading.

Freeze Feature

Locks in the Roto Brush selections to ensure consistency throughout the animation by preventing recalculations.

Timeline Panel

The Timeline was essential for stacking layers, adjusting durations, and syncing transitions.
