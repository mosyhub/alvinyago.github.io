History of Windows 95

Windows 95 is a landmark operating system developed by Microsoft, officially released on August 24, 1995. It marked a major shift from the older MS-DOS-based systems to a more user-friendly and visually appealing graphical interface.

Windows 95 brought plug and play hardware support, making it easier to install new devices, and featured built-in networking capabilities, allowing easier internet and LAN access. Its blend of usability and modern architecture made it a milestone in the evolution of operating systems.
